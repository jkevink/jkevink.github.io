Understanding the relationship between free amino acids, fatty acids, and TBARS value

One of the aim of this study was to understand the relationship free amino acids and fatty acids with TBARS. In a nutshell, the higher the TBARS value, the more oxidation species and flavours would appear in the products. Numerous factors were explored here from different lamb cuts and High Pressure Processing (HPP) processing parameters. A RM-ANOVA is applied here as chemical measures are done in triplicates and Fisher LSD was then applied. Multiple PCAs (not shown here) was also made but to further understand the overall relationship a MFA was then conducted. The results of this study is published in Foods in 2020.
