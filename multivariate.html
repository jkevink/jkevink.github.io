What drives wine complexity?

The aim this study was to look at how wine complexity varying with type and age may perceived differently between novices and expert tasters. The method that was used here was Temporal Check All That Apply which means that participants were asked to provide their temporal perception of the wine. We also looked on what characteristics of the wine drives complexity. The results of this study is published in Food Quality and Preference in 2021.
